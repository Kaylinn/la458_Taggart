Refugee Crisis Along the Balkan Route

This map shows the current migration numbers along the Balkan Refugee Route for Refugees, Asylum-Seekers, IDPs, Returnees, Stateless Persons, and other people. The Balkan Route is one of the major routes which refugees take when fleeing. The route to safety is dangerous, with blockades, military patrol, and increasing violence.

Note: Map data is pulled from a geoJSON file using UNHCR datasets and MSF.org.

  • Click on the purple markers to see April, 2018 migration statistics in each highlighted country.
  • Under layer control, you can change the base layers, as well as overlay markers where Doctors Without Borders (MSF) are currently working. MSF works in many othe places around the world but for the focus of this project, I will only be looking at locations around the Balkan Route.
